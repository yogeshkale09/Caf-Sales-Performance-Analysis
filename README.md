## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing transactional sales data from a multi-location café to uncover business insights and support data-driven decision-making. The dataset initially contained inconsistencies, missing values, and formatting issues, which were resolved through structured data cleaning and transformation. The final output is a 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard designed using advanced DAX measures and professional visualization techniques.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Perform end-to-end data cleaning and transformation
* Build a scalable data model using best practices
* Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)
* Identify key business trends and performance drivers
* Deliver actionable insights and recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** (Data Modeling, DAX, Visualization)
* **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* **DAX** (Measures & Calculations)

---

## 🔄 Data Cleaning Steps

* Replaced invalid values like *UNKNOWN* and *ERROR* with nulls
* Handled missing values using logical imputation
* Validated pricing using: Total Spent ÷ Quantity
* Converted columns into appropriate data types
* Removed duplicate and inconsistent records
* Standardized categorical fields (Location, Item, Payment Method)

---

## 📊 Data Modeling

* Created a **Date Table** for time intelligence analysis
* Established **One-to-Many relationship** between Date and Transaction data
* Built calculated columns for Day, Month, and Customer Segmentation

### 2️⃣ Sales & Product Analysis

* Top vs Bottom Products

### 3️⃣ Customer Behavior & Trends

* Sales by Day of Week
* Weekend vs Weekday Analysis

* Average Spend by Location
* Payment Behavior Analysis

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* Revenue is concentrated among a few high-performing products (Pareto effect)
* Significant variation in performance across locations
* Digital payments dominate customer transactions
* Weekend sales outperform weekdays consistently
* Mid-week sales show noticeable dips in customer engagement

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on promoting top-performing products to maximize revenue
* Optimize pricing strategies for high-volume, low-revenue items
* Improve low-performing locations with targeted marketing
* Increase staffing and inventory during peak periods (weekends)
* Introduce weekday promotions to boost mid-week sales
* Enhance digital payment experience and offer incentives
